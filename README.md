# Cartel Dashboard

### We give offline Indian shops an AI brain that catches expiring stock prices it and broadcasts deals

## Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate vast amounts of operational data every day through billing systems, inventory management software, and manual record-keeping. However, converting this data into meaningful business decisions remains a significant challenge.

Through merchant interviews conducted with **Aadhirai Mart**, **Ratnadeep Mart**, and **Bakery**, we identified a recurring problem:

> Existing retail software excels at recording transactions but provides limited support for decision-making.

Store owners and inventory managers spend valuable time manually reviewing reports to identify slow-moving inventory, expiring products, promotional opportunities, and customer engagement strategies. As a result, inventory interventions are delayed, marketing opportunities are missed, and operational efficiency depends heavily on experience rather than data-driven insights.

---

## Our Solution

**Cartel Dashboard** is an AI-powered intelligence layer designed to work alongside existing retail systems.

Instead of replacing current workflows, Cartel connects with existing merchant data and automatically transforms operational records into actionable recommendations, marketing campaigns, and business insights.

The platform enables merchants to move from:

**Data → Insights → Actions**

within minutes, without requiring technical expertise, database knowledge, or complex software adoption.

# How It Works

### Step 1

Merchant connects existing business data or uploads exported reports.

### Step 2

Cartel analyzes inventory, sales, and operational information.

### Step 3

AI identifies products and business areas requiring attention.

### Step 4

The platform recommends interventions such as:

* Stock clearance actions
* Expiry management
* Promotional opportunities
* Customer engagement campaigns

### Step 5

Merchants review, approve, and execute recommendations directly from the dashboard.
